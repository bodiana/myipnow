2. Enable Split Tunneling

Split tunneling allows you to choose which apps use the VPN and which connect directly to the internet. This is particularly useful for:

  • Reducing bandwidth usage for non-sensitive activities.

Common Issues and Troubleshooting Tips

Even with top-notch services like Ipvanish, users may run into issues. Here are some common problems and their solutions:

1. Slow Connection Speeds

If you're experiencing slow speeds, try the following:

  • Switch to a different server, preferably one closer to your location.
  • Disable unnecessary applications consuming bandwidth.
  • Check your internet speed without the VPN to ensure the issue isn't with your internet service provider.

2. Inability to Access Certain Content

Sometimes, even with a VPN, you may find that certain content is still blocked. Consider these steps:

  • Try connecting to a different server; some may be more effective for specific geo-restrictions.
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies, as this can sometimes resolve access issues.

3. Frequent Disconnections

If you face frequent disconnections, try:

  • Changing your VPN protocol in the settings. Some protocols may offer better stability than others.
  • Reinstalling the Ipvanish app to resolve any potential software glitches.
