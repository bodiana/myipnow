What Is a WHOIS Lookup?

A WHOIS lookup shows public registration data for a domain ‚Äî typically the registrar, important dates (creation/expiry), name servers, and registration status. Frequently Asked Questions

What is WHOIS Lookup?
WHOIS Lookup retrieves registration information of a domain including registrar, creation and expiry dates, nameservers, and owner information.
