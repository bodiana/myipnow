Top 30 Most Blocked IP Ranges Observed in Public Blacklists

Aggregated, statistical overview of IP ranges that most frequently appear in public blacklist datasets. This page reports patterns, not individual IP addresses.
Last updated: January 2026

At-a-Glance Highlights

  • Cloud and hosting infrastructure dominates blacklist frequency observations.
  • Spam, proxy abuse, and automated traffic are the most common categories.
  • Data is aggregated by IP range and ASN; no individual IPs are listed.

How This Data Was Compiled

This dataset aggregates observations from multiple publicly available IP reputation and DNS-based blacklists. Results are grouped by IP range and ASN to identify patterns in listing frequency across different providers and regions.

Frequency values are expressed qualitatively (High / Medium / Low) to avoid overinterpretation of raw counts and to ensure responsible data usage.
